I started wrestling in 8th grade because my friend said I should try it. I figured why not give it a shot being that I was not playing any sports that year. My mom told me I did it when I was younger and didnt like it, so I didnt have high expectaions going in. I quickly feel in love with the sport. I wasnt good but I enjoyed learning new moves and wrestling other teams. Once I got into High School I knew I was going to wrestle and so was my friend. I was told the first week of High School Wrestling is the hardest week, then it gets easier. The first day we went outside to the track and ran a mile, then ran up and down the bleachers about 2 times. I thought the rest of practice would be easier but I was wrong. We came in and got our shoes on and ran more, then drilled basic moves as hard as we could. The practice ran for about 3 hours. The next day we did the same thing exepct this time we ran 2 miles and ran the bleachers 4 times. We slowed down with the running after that but everyday we go to the 3rd floor and run for about 15-30 minutes, then start practice.

All Me ~Nick Vitelli
